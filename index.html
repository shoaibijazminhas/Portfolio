About Ch Farm City

Know more about our farming journey

Passionate Farming Since 2020

Shoaib Ijaz Minhas is a dedicated farmer based in Village Rabal, Chakwal. With over 50 Bigha of agricultural land, we grow a wide variety of fresh, organic vegetables and supply them daily to the local Mandi.

Our commitment is to provide high-quality, pesticide-free vegetables to our community while promoting sustainable farming practices.
